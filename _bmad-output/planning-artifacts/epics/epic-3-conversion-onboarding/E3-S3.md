# E3-S3 — Customer list/detail with lineage + timeline

> **GitHub Epic → Feature → Story:** Epic **E3 — Conversion Saga & Customer Onboarding** › Feature **3.3 — Customer List & Detail** › Story **E3-S3**
> **Cut:** Pilot · **Depends on:** **E0-S1** (`CUSTOMER_TRANSITIONS` / `STATUS_TONE`) · **E0-S4** (repository reads/4-beat), **E0-S6** (record-view permission gate), **E0-S7** (domain-event stream the timeline reads) · E3-S1 (customer record + lineage pointers)

## Story
As a user, I view customers and trace each back to its originating lead, so that I can see a customer's full provenance and activity in one place.

## Implementation-Ready Acceptance Criteria

1. **AC1 — `ListPage` + `DetailPage` with customer-toned `StatusPill`.** Customers render on the shared `ListPage` template and a `DetailPage`; the `StatusPill` shows the four customer states including `prospect`/`onboarding`, colored only from `STATUS_TONE` (no hex/px) (NFR-10).
   *Touches:* `src/features/customers/CustomerListPage.tsx`, `src/features/customers/CustomerDetailPage.tsx`, `src/shared/ui/{templates/ListPage, templates/DetailPage, components/StatusPill}` · *Seam:* `Repository<Customer>.list/get` · *Schema/Map:* `STATUS_TONE` for customer states.

2. **AC2 — Detail shows lineage + activity timeline (ADR-016).** The DetailPage shows **lineage** — a link to the originating lead via `convertedFromLeadId` — and the **Activity timeline** = the `DomainEvent` stream filtered by `entityType=Customer + entityId`, **gated by permission to view the record** (ADR-016 timeline split; the viewer is included within scope).
   *Touches:* `src/features/customers/CustomerDetailPage.tsx`, `src/features/tickets/ActivityTimeline.tsx` (shared component), `src/shared/auth/permissions.ts` · *Seam:* `DomainEvent` stream read + `can()` record-view gate · *Schema/Map:* event filter `entityType+entityId`.

3. **AC3 — Related Tickets tab (feeds Epic 4).** The DetailPage exposes a related Tickets tab scoped to the customer; in this story it is the seam/placeholder that Epic 4 populates (no ticket CRUD here).
   *Touches:* `src/features/customers/CustomerDetailPage.tsx` · *Seam:* `Repository<Ticket>.list({ customerId })` (consumed by Epic 4) · *Schema/Map:* n/a.

4. **AC4 — Four states, scoped (UC-1, UC-5).** List and detail render loading/empty/error/data via `<QueryStateBoundary>`; all reads are scoped from auth context only — out-of-tenant access → `404` (no existence leak).
   *Touches:* `src/features/customers/{CustomerListPage.tsx, CustomerDetailPage.tsx}`, `src/shared/ui/QueryStateBoundary.tsx`, `src/shared/auth/guards.tsx` · *Seam:* `Repository<Customer>` scoped reads + route guard · *Schema/Map:* n/a.

5. **AC5 — Customer create/edit mutation via `CustomerForm` (UC-2; backs the C-3 activation gate).** A Zod-validated `CustomerForm` (shared `EntityForm`) creates and edits a customer through the 4-beat: validate-before-persist (invalid → `422` + field details), then emit exactly one `Customer.Created` / `Customer.Updated` `DomainEvent` + one `AuditEvent` under one `correlationId` (UC-2); optimistic with rollback on error. **Edit is the only way to set `taxRegistrationNumber` / `contactAddress`** (DEC-CC-2), so this AC is the mechanism that makes the **E3-S2 `onboarding → active` activation gate** satisfiable ("Edit the customer to fill it in, then activate"). Fields in order: Name (req) · Primary email (req, validated) · Phone · Owner (req) · Tax registration number · Contact address.
   *Touches:* `src/features/customers/{CustomerForm.tsx, customer.service.ts}`, `src/shared/domain/{customer.types.ts, schemas.ts}`, `src/shared/ui/templates/EntityForm`, `src/shared/events/{bus.ts,auditLog.ts,correlation.ts}` · *Seam:* 4-beat (authorize→mutate→emit→audit) · *Schema/Map:* `customerCreateSchema`/`customerUpdateSchema` (Zod); canonical `Customer.Created`/`Customer.Updated`.

6. **AC6 — Role gate: C-2 kept — `support` is READ-ONLY on customers.** Per conflict **C-2 (ruled keep constitution)**, customer **create/edit** is granted to `tenant_admin` / `sales` only; `support` and `viewer` are **read-only** on customer fields (read-only roles show a "Read-only" pill, no Edit affordance). **Do NOT replicate the prototype's wider `canWrite` grant** (the prototype lets `support` edit customers — that is the divergence we deliberately do not build). `support` still **reads** customers and may drive the **status transition** for onboarding (E3-S2) — that is a separate capability from field-edit. The Edit/New-customer affordance is gated by the `can()` action guard (E0-S6).
   *Touches:* `src/features/customers/CustomerForm.tsx`, `src/shared/auth/permissions.ts`, `src/shared/auth/guards.tsx` · *Seam:* two-gate authZ on create/edit · *Schema/Map:* §2.2 "Customers: create/edit" row (admin/sales write; support/viewer read — C-2 kept).

## UX & Behavior (from prototype)
*Source: `prototype/app/customers.jsx` (`CustomerDetail`, `CustomerForm`), `prototype/app/templates.jsx` (`ENTITY.customers`). Visual oracle: `prototype/screenshots/cust-detail.png`. Build identical to the prototype.*

- **List columns in order:** **Account** (building icon + name) · **Status** (customer `StatusPill`) · **Primary email** · **Phone**. Filters: Status + Owner dropdowns, search over name/email, column sort, **25/page**, URL-reflected. Five states incl. filtered-empty (same as Leads). Create CTA **"New customer"** (role-gated).
- **`CustomerForm` modal — fields in order:** Name (required) · **Primary email** (required, validated) · Phone · Owner (required) · **Tax registration number** · **Contact address**. Info strip: *"Tax registration number and contact address are optional for a prospect, but **both are required to activate** the customer."* *(Both fields are **ratified** — DEC-CC-2; they back the E3-S2 activation gate.)* Create/edit is a 4-beat mutation (`Customer.Created`/`Customer.Updated` + audit, one `correlationId`; `422` on invalid) — see **AC5**.
- **⚠️ C-2 (kept constitution) — `support` is READ-ONLY on customers.** Build the Edit / "New customer" affordances **gated to `tenant_admin`/`sales` only**; `support`/`viewer` are read-only (see **AC6**). The prototype's `canWrite` customers includes `support` — that wider grant is the divergence we deliberately **do not build**. Support keeps customer *read* + the onboarding *status transition* (E3-S2), which is a separate capability.
- **Detail header:** breadcrumb, back, title, **customer `StatusPill`**, role-gated **Edit** (admin/sales only — C-2) + **Change status**; read-only roles (support/viewer) → **"Read-only"** pill.
- **Two-column body.** Left: details grid (Name, Primary email, Phone, Tax registration number, Contact address — DEC-CC-2) + a **tabbed card**:
  - **Activity** tab → `RecordTimeline` that **interleaves conversion lineage + status changes + ticket lifecycle** in one stream; lineage entries carry a **"Lineage"** tag, ticket entries a **"Ticket"** tag.
  - **Tickets** tab → count badge, **"New ticket for this customer"** (if `canCreate` ticket), and a list of the customer's tickets (subject, assignee, priority + status pills) linking to ticket detail; empty state otherwise. *(In this story the tab is the seam Epic 4 populates.)*
- **Right rail — Lineage card:** if converted, a green **"Converted from lead <name>"** button opening the originating lead (or **404** if the lead is out of scope); else *"No originating lead — created directly."* Then **Owner** ("Account owner") and **Meta** cards.
- *Oracle:* `cust-detail.png` (Helix Labs · Active · "Converted from lead Avery Stone" · owner Marco Ruiz).

## Test Requirements (NFR-12)
- **Vitest:** customer-state → `STATUS_TONE` mapping (incl. `prospect`/`onboarding`); lineage resolver returns the correct originating lead from `convertedFromLeadId`; timeline filter selects only `Customer/{id}` DomainEvents; record-view gate (`can()`) admits in-scope viewer, denies out-of-scope. **Create/edit (AC5):** `customerCreate/UpdateSchema` validation (valid/invalid → `422` + field detail); an accepted create/edit emits exactly one `Customer.Created`/`Customer.Updated` + one `AuditEvent` sharing one `correlationId`; editing sets `taxRegistrationNumber`/`contactAddress` and satisfies the E3-S2 activation precondition. **Permission cells (AC6 / C-2):** create/edit granted to `tenant_admin`/`sales`, **denied to `support`/`viewer`** (`Auth.RoleDenied`); `support` retains customer *read* + the onboarding status transition.
- **RTL:** list + detail render four states; lineage link navigates to the originating lead; Tickets tab renders as an (empty) seam; StatusPill uses tones not hex; `CustomerForm` renders four states and rolls back optimistically on injected fault; the Edit/"New customer" affordance is **absent/disabled for `support`/`viewer`** (read-only pill shown).
- **Playwright:** open a converted customer, follow the lineage link back to its lead, confirm the linked conversion event appears on the customer timeline; out-of-tenant customer URL → `404`.

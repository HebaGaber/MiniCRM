# E2-S3 вЂ” Walk a lead through its lifecycle

> **GitHub Epic в†’ Feature в†’ Story:** Epic **E2 вЂ” Leads: Capture в†’ Qualify** вЂє Feature **2.3 вЂ” Qualification State Machine** вЂє Story **E2-S3**

## Story
As a salesperson, I move a lead `new в†’ contacted в†’ qualified | disqualified`, with revive, so that the pipeline reflects each lead's real qualification state under enforced rules.

## Implementation-Ready Acceptance Criteria
1. **AC1 вЂ” Only LEAD_TRANSITIONS moves allowed; illegal в†’ 422, pill unchanged (UJ-2 edge case, UC-3).** Status moves go through `POST /leads/{id}/transition` and are validated by `canTransition(lead, from, to)` against `LEAD_TRANSITIONS`. An illegal move (e.g. `new в†’ qualified`) resolves as `422`; the lead's status is **not** mutated and the `StatusPill` stays unchanged. Legal moves: `newв†’[contacted,disqualified]`, `contactedв†’[qualified,disqualified]`, `qualifiedв†’[converted,disqualified]`.
   *Touches:* `src/features/leads/lead.service.ts`, `src/shared/domain/status.ts`, `src/shared/data/LocalStorageRepository.ts` В· *Seam:* 4-beat **mutate** (transition guard) В· *Schema/Map:* `LEAD_TRANSITIONS` + `canTransition`.
2. **AC2 вЂ” Revive: disqualified в†’ contacted allowed.** `disqualified в†’ contacted` is a legal move per the map (revive); after revive the lead is workable again and may proceed `contacted в†’ qualified | disqualified`.
   *Touches:* `src/shared/domain/status.ts`, `src/features/leads/lead.service.ts` В· *Seam:* 4-beat **mutate** В· *Schema/Map:* `LEAD_TRANSITIONS` (`disqualifiedв†’[contacted]`).
3. **AC3 вЂ” Each accepted change emits Lead.StatusChanged + audit, one correlationId (UC-2).** Every accepted transition runs the full 4-beat: **authorize** (`can()` for `lead`/`transition`) в†’ **mutate** (guard + `version` bump в†’ `409` on mismatch) в†’ **emit** one `Lead.StatusChanged` (carrying from/to) в†’ **audit** one immutable `AuditEvent`, all threaded by a single `correlationId`. Uses `POST /leads/{id}/transition`, **never** `PATCH status`.
   *Touches:* `src/features/leads/lead.service.ts`, `src/shared/data/LocalStorageRepository.ts`, `src/shared/events/{bus.ts,auditLog.ts,correlation.ts}`, `src/shared/auth/permissions.ts` В· *Seam:* 4-beat (authorizeв†’mutateв†’emitв†’audit) В· *Schema/Map:* canonical `Lead.StatusChanged`; `LEAD_TRANSITIONS`.
4. **AC4 вЂ” `qualified` is a manual judgment call (flag J/K).** Reaching `qualified` is an explicit human action (`contacted в†’ qualified`); there is no automatic scoring or rule that promotes a lead. The transition is gated only by the map and the role guard, not by any computed BANT value.
   *Touches:* `src/features/leads/lead.service.ts`, `src/shared/domain/status.ts` В· *Seam:* 4-beat **mutate** В· *Schema/Map:* `LEAD_TRANSITIONS` (`contactedв†’qualified` manual).

## UX & Behavior (from prototype)
*Source: `prototype/app/shared_records.jsx` (`ChangeStatusControl`), `prototype/app/store.jsx` (`TRANSITIONS.lead`). This is the single shared state-machine UI for leads/customers/tickets вЂ” build identical to the prototype.*

- **`ChangeStatusControl`** вЂ” the button is **disabled when the current status is terminal** (title *"<Status> is terminal вЂ” read-only"*; e.g. `converted`). Otherwise it opens a dropdown.
- **Dropdown lists only legal next states** under **"Move this lead to"**, each rendered as a pill with a `в†’`.
- **Illegal states are shown but locked** вЂ” `lock` icon, `cursor: not-allowed`, grouped under a **"Not available from <Status>"** header. Attempting a locked target surfaces an inline **warning panel** stating the **rule + next legal step** вЂ” **no StatusPill tone change, no toast, and no event** (422 discipline / DEC-UX-6). Focus stays on the control; the offered next legal action is reachable in one tab.
- **Legal transition:** the move applies optimistically; the **StatusPill cross-fades its tone token at `--crm-fast`** (lead tones: new `neutral`, contacted/qualified `info`в†’`success`, disqualified `danger`, converted `success`); a sentence is appended to the lead's Activity timeline. Tone comes **only** from `STATUS_TONE`; pill text is the enum label.
- **Lead transition map (matches constitution В§3.2 exactly):** `newв†’[contacted,disqualified]`, `contactedв†’[qualified,disqualified]`, `qualifiedв†’[converted,disqualified]`, `disqualifiedв†’[contacted]` (revive), `convertedв†’[]` (terminal).
- **Rollback:** if an accepted optimistic move fails server-side, the pill snaps back at `--crm-slow` + toast (generic / 409 distinct).
